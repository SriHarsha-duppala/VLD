SSC CHSL Recruitment 2025 - Apply Online for 3131 LDC, DEO and More Posts
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) will release the SSC CHSL 2025 notification on June 23, 2025, along with the commencement of the online application process. The last date to apply online is July 18, 2025. The Tier 1 exam is scheduled from September 8 to 18, 2025. The SSC CHSL exam is conducted annually to recruit candidates for various positions like Lower Division Clerk, Junior Secretariat Assistant, Postal Assistant, and Data Entry Operator.
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Recruitment 2025 for posts of 3131 CHSL. Candidates with 12TH Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 23-06-2025, and closes on 18-07-2025. The candidate shall apply online through SSC website, ssc.gov.in.

Name of the Post: SSC CHSL Online Form 2025

Application Fee
  • The SSC CHSL application fee is INR 100 for General/ OBC/ EWS candidates.
  • There is no fee for female candidates of all categories, and candidates belonging to SC, ST, PwD, and Ex-Servicemen categories
