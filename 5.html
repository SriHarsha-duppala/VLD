UPSC CDS II Recruitment Notification 2025 - Apply Online for 453 Posts
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 453 CDS II posts, and interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official UPSC website before the last date of 17-06-2025, with this article providing complete details including eligibility criteria, age limit, salary structure, selection process, application steps, and direct links to the official notification and online application form.
The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Recruitment 2025 for 453 posts of CDS II. Candidates with Any Graduate, B.Tech/B.E Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 28-05-2025, and closes on 17-06-2025. The candidate shall apply online through UPSC website, upsconline.nic.in.
