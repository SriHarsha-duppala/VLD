Central Bank of India Apprentices Recruitment 2025 - Apply Online for 4500 Posts
The Central Bank of India has released an official notification for the recruitment of 4500 Apprentices posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official Central Bank of India website. The last date to submit the application form is 23-06-2025.
The Central Bank of India Recruitment 2025 for 4500 posts of Apprentices. Candidates with Any Graduate Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 07-06-2025, and closes on 23-06-2025. The candidate shall apply online through Central Bank of India website, centralbankofindia.co.in.
