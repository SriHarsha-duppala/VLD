ISRO ICRB Scientist/ Engineer Recruitment 2025 - Apply Online for 320 Posts
The Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) has released a notification for the recruitment of 320 Scientist/Engineer posts, and interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official ISRO website by 16-06-2025, with this article providing complete information on eligibility criteria, age limit, salary structure, selection process, application steps, and direct links to the official notification and online application form.
The Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) Recruitment 2025 for 320 posts of Scientist/ Engineer. Candidates with B.Tech/B.E Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 27-05-2025, and closes on 16-06-2025. The candidate shall apply online through ISRO website, isro.gov.in.
Name of the Post: ISRO Scientist/ Engineer Online Form 2025
Post Date:27-05-2025
Latest update: 28-05-2025
Total Vacancy: 320

Application Fee
  • For all Posts: Rs. 250/-
  • Initially all candidates have to uniformly pay: Rs. 750/- per application as processing fee
  • Rs. 750/-: i.e. refund in full for candidates who are exempted from payment of Application Fee (women, SC/ST/ PwBD and Ex-Servicemen).
  • Rs. 500/-:i.e. after deducting the Application Fee in respect of all other candidates.
