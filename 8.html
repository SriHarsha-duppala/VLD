RRB Technician Recruitment 2025 - Apply Online for 6180 Posts
The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 6180 Technician posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official RRB website. The last date to submit the application form is 28-07-2025.
A recent press release has announced the tentative RRB Technician vacancies for the 2025–26 recruitment cycle. As per the intent notice, a total of 6180 Technician Grade 1 and Grade 3 vacancies across 51 categories are expected to be released, based on assessments conducted by all zonal railways. This recruitment drive presents a golden opportunity for candidates, especially those who missed last year’s cycle, as such notifications are not frequently issued by the Indian Railways.

Although the official notification is yet to be published on the RRB website, the indent reply confirms the upcoming vacancies, with South Eastern Railway (SER) having the highest at 1,215 and East Central Railway (ECR) the lowest at 31. Indian Railways, comprising 18 zones and multiple Production Units, has approved these tentative vacancies, and the detailed Centralised Employment Notification (CEN) is expected to be released soon across all regional RRB websites. Candidates are advised to stay updated, as the final vacancy details will be officially confirmed in the upcoming notification.
