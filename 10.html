SSC MTS Havaldar Recruitment 2025 - Apply Online for 1075 Posts
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the official notification for SSC Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) and Havaldar Recruitment 2025 on June 26, 2025. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official SSC website. The last date to submit the application form is 24-07-2025.
The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) Recruitment 2025 for posts of Multi Tasking Staff, Havaldar. Candidates with 10TH Can Apply Online. The online application opens on 26-06-2025, and closes on 24-07-2025. The candidate shall apply online through SSC website, ssc.gov.in.
